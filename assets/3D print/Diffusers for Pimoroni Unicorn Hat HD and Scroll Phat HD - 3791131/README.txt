Diffusers for Pimoroni Unicorn Hat HD and Scroll Phat HD by ali1234 on Thingiverse: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3791131

Summary:
This is a diffuser for Pimoroni Scroll Phat HD and Unicorn Hat HD. It comprises two parts: a mesh grid to keep the pixels sharp and a flat front plate for the light to project on to. Print the mesh in black and the front in whatever colour you want (white recommended for the Unicorn HD). There is also a back plate with and without slot for gpio pins.
